Meet Your Attorney

David Glik, Esq.

For over two decades, Attorney David Glik has been one of Boston's most trusted advocates for immigrants, those facing criminal charges, and individuals whose civil rights have been violated. A graduate of Boston University School of Law, he founded Glik & Associates in 2003 with one mission: to provide the same quality of legal defense to working families that was previously only available to the wealthy.

Attorney Glik is known for his aggressive courtroom presence, meticulous preparation, and genuine compassion for every client he represents. He speaks English, Spanish, and Portuguese — ensuring language is never a barrier to justice. When you hire Glik & Associates, you work directly with Attorney Glik from your first call to the final resolution of your case.
